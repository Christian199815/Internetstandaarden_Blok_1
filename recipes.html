THE RECIPES

  • Cuba Libre

    The most famous party cocktail with a different name, that makes sense if you look at what it contains.

    Amount Fluid
    2 wedge Lime (fresh)
    2 shot Havana Club 3 Year Old rum
    1 dash Angostura Aromatic Bitters
    4 shot Cola (e.g. Coca Cola or Pepsi Cola)

  • Espresso Martini

    The drink with the well phrased "shaken not stirred".

    Amount Fluid
    11/2 shot Ketel One Vodka
    1 shot Espresso coffee (freshly made & hot)
    2/3 shot Bebo Coffee Liqueur
    2 drop Saline solution 4:1

  • Dark 'n Stormy

    A sweet but bitter starter drink for the one who likes something darker before the storm of the night starts.

    Amount Fluid
    12/3 shot Dark/black/blackstrap Rum
    2/3 shot Lime juice (freshly squeezed)
    1/3 shot Sugar syrup 'rich'
    2 dash Angostura Aromatic Bitters
    3 shot Thomas Henry Ginger Beer
